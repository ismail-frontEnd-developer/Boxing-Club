OUR APPROACH TO FITNESS.

Our approach to fitness training differs from the run of the mill globo-gyms and fitness studios out there that people are used to. We do not believe in artificial climate control, muscle isolation and machine assisted workouts, mirrors and color coded shakers. Our units are ‘no frills’, ‘no gimmicks’ and very spartan. We don’t do fancy; We do effective!


The basic fitness program component is only 30 minutes a day for 5 days a week but will give you martial arts based fitness foundation to move into any sport, activity or challenge and hit the ground running. We use High as well as low intensity, high volume full-body combat fitness with complex movements and completely scalable progressions. Our periodisation and programming is based on solid sports science and time tested martial arts strengthening components. Dont use machines…. Be the machine!


Combat Kinetics is all about running, sprinting, jumping, crawling, throwing, climbing, rolling and having complete control over your body and mind. We work on more than just isolated strength, machine assisted endurance and bulky muscles. We don’t have trainers, we have coaches. We have combined the latest aspects of sports science with gymnastics, yoga, martial arts, calisthenics and strongman workouts to provide you with a program which flawlessly combines the best aspects of each of these systems with the other.


The result? True strength, extreme mobility, explosive power, elite endurance and a superbly balanced physique. Our General Physical Preparedness(GPP) programs have provided the fitness base for over 100 national and international medals, including World and Asian level championships across multiple combat sports.

OUR APPROACH TO BLACK EAGLE.


MMA Combat Sport:

We take our combat seriously. We do not waste our time with useless formalities and rituals. We train recreational, amateur and professional athletes in full contact, competitive combat sports at the highest levels.


Whether our students take their skills into competition or not, we expect discipline, dedication and hard work. Our MMA and Submission Grappling syllabus consists of elements from Sambo, Muay Thai, Judo, Bjj, Catch Wrestling and Boxing.


We give you Elite level skills, train a champion’s mindset and apply unmatched sports science based programming, You will not find any training system that compares to us.


MMA Tactical Combat:

Our tactical combatives module for advanced students builds on our Competitive MMA syllabus to include lethal striking techniques, small joint manipulation, knife, stick and small weapon skills.


For institutional requirements for government agencies, defense forces and law and order we can bring in tactical Sambo instructors from our Russian counterparts to teach Close Quarter Battle, Firearms, Sentry Termination and other specialized modules.
